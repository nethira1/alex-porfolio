FinTrack Dashboard Redesign

Challenge

200,000+ users were struggling with a fragmented dashboard — key actions were buried 3+ taps deep, leading to high drop-off in core financial tracking flows.

Approach

Conducted 14 user interviews and analysed session recordings to identify friction points. Built a revised information architecture with progressive disclosure, prioritising the top 3 user goals on the home screen.

Outcome

38% reduction in task-completion time. Net Promoter Score rose from 32 to 58 in three months post-launch. The redesigned dashboard became the product team's template for subsequent feature work.

🛍️

Bloom Shop — Mobile Experience

Challenge

Bloom's mobile site had a 74% cart-abandonment rate and poor accessibility scores, costing significant revenue each month.

Approach

End-to-end mobile redesign focused on one-thumb reachability, streamlined checkout (reduced from 6 steps to 3), and WCAG AA compliance. Ran five rounds of usability testing with the target customer segment.

Outcome

Conversion rate increased by 24%. Accessibility score jumped from 61 to 96. Featured as a case study at a major sustainable fashion conference.

Leafy — Component Library

Challenge

Three product teams were duplicating UI work, leading to inconsistent experiences and wasted engineering time across a growing SaaS platform.

Approach

Audited existing UI patterns across all products, identified 60 reusable components, and built a Figma library alongside a React/Storybook implementation with full documentation and accessibility annotations.

Outcome

New-feature development time cut by 50%. Design-to-engineer handoff time reduced from days to hours. The library is now the foundation for all new product work at the company.
